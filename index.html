ABOUT ME

Websites done right.

I create websites and applications following 3 principles:

Simplicity

Things are simple, people makes them complicated. It's very important to desing websites and applications with simplicity in order to keep the focus for what it was designed for. Unrequired extras can ruin a project.

Functionality

Keeping in mind the simplicity, functionality is the next step. Nobody likes to press a button or a link and nothing happens. Everyone loves sites and apps that loads fast, works as expected and are easy to use.

Elegance

The elegance is the capstone of every project. It will determine the identity of the project with the right combination of colors, design, navigation and utilities. The goal is an efficient and pleasant development.
